 Franken Archives Moved
The archives at franken.de have been moved to ftp.uni-erlangen.de to support the increase in download traffic from the Cygwin areas. ftp.uni-erlangen.de has a 155Mbit connection so the change should be a good thing for all users. Please check all your links and make sure they reflect the new name. The upload of ported packages is still allowed to the upload directory on ftp.franken.de as described in my package report. Apr 19 2001, Michael Hirmke
