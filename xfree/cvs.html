Cygwin/XFree86 server source and selected client sources are distributed with the XFree86 Project's CVS tree. Anonymous access to the XFree86 tree is available, simple follow the instructions on the XFree86 CVS page.

See the Cygwin/XFree86 Contributor's Guide for information that will help you contribute to the Cygwin/XFree86 project, such as compilation tutorials, compilation tips, expected build times, overviews of the technologies involved, and a full bibliography.

Additional general information and documention for CVS is available from the CVS Home site.
