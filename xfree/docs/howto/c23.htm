Cygwin/XFree Contents

Cygwin/XFree consist of two parts: Cygwin, a port of the GNU tools to Win32, and Cygwin/XFree, a port of XFree86 to Win32. Both parts of Cygwin/XFree are needed to successfully run an X/Server on a Win32 platform.
