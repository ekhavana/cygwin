Cygwin/X - Chat (IRC)

Last updated: 2004-01-08 1210 EST

Cygwin/X developers (Harold, Alexander Gottwald, Kensuke Matsuzaki, and others) often hang out in IRC chat rooms and can offer assistance or just general chat about Cygwin/X. Our primary channel is #cygwinx on freenode. Harold additionally hangs out in #freedesktop and #xwin on freenode, where you are likely to see conversations about implementing new Cygwin/X features taking place with others from the X community.
