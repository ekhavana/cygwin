Cygwin/X developers (Harold L Hunt II [huntharo], Alexander Gottwald [ago1024], Kensuke Matsuzaki [zakki], and others) often hang out in IRC chat rooms and can offer assistance or just general chat about Cygwin/X. Our primary channel is #cygwinx (note the "x" on the end) on freenode. Harold additionally hangs out in #freedesktop and #xwin on freenode, where you are likely to see conversations about implementing new Cygwin/X features taking place with others from the X community.
