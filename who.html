Who are we?

The Cygwin group at Cygnus Solutions consists primarily of three people: DJ Delorie, Christopher Faylor, and Geoffrey Noer.

Like many Sourceware projects, contributions from net people are especially important. Cygwin benefits from thousands of testers and dozens of contributors, including some incredible people like Mumit Khan and Sergey Okhapkin.

Cygnus Solutions is the leader in Open Source-based software development tools, mission critical support, and custom engineering for the embedded systems market. Our products include:

  • eCos, the embedded Cygnus operating system.
  • GNUPro Toolkit, development tools for 125 desktop and cross-platform development environments.
  • Source Navigator, a powerful source code comprehension tool.
