Who are we?

The Cygwin group at Red Hat, Inc. consists primarily of three people: Christopher Faylor (manager), DJ Delorie and Corinna Vinschen (senior engineers).

Cygwin was originally designed and implemented by Steve Chamberlain when he worked for Cygnus.

Geoffrey Noer spearheaded Cygwin development at Cygnus for almost three years from 1996 - 1999. He provided many useful innovations to Cygwin as well as sheparding most of Cygwin's development during that period.

Like many free software projects, contributions from net people are especially important. Cygwin benefits from thousands of testers and dozens of contributors, including some incredible people like Mumit Khan, Sergey Okhapkin, Earnie Boyd, and Egor Duda. (If you know of other contributors who you think should be listed here, please let us know)

The Engineering Services branch of Red Hat, Inc. is a leader in Open Source-based software development tools, mission critical support, and custom engineering for the embedded systems market. Our products include:

  • eCos, the embedded Cygnus operating system.
  • GNUPro Toolkit, development tools for 125 desktop and cross-platform development environments.
  • Source Navigator, a powerful source code comprehension tool.
