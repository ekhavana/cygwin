Cygwin GNOME 1.4 binary packages

I've setup a project on sourceforge.net named cygnome, aiming to port GNOME desktop to Cygwin. Currently most of GNOME 1.4 packages are available, including core libraries, the GNOME desktop, Sawfish window manager, gnumeric and anjuta. But Nautilus is not available now. Most of these are built with the patches and hints contributed by Steven O'Brien at http://homepage.ntlworld.com/steven.obrien2. With his great work, this port come to reality. I'm providing these binary packages just in hope that it could be useful, esp. for those guys who have less time to build it by themselves. Dec 10 2002, Hansom Young
